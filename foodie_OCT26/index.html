Meet Foodie

Our smart, uplifting rover delivers food and drinks from local restaurants when hunger strikes.
Whether you’re at the office or working from home, Foodie will happily travel to you.

Features

Exterior

Foodie’s outer shell is made from durable material for resistance against weather and physical damage while on its journey to its desination. A 360 camera on its back helps with navigation and monitoring its surrounding area for added security.

Interior

Foodie’s storage is separated into two chambers for hot and cold foods and drinks. Materials such as polyethelyne terephthalate (PET) is used to keep your food safe from harmful chemicals in high and low temperatures.

Foodie Moodies

Besides being a delivery robot, Foodie also provides emotional support for its community. When traveling, you can find Foodie singing a tune on a sunny day or making heart eyes when a customer is happy with their order.

Order

For Customers

For Businesses

For Customers

  1. Open the app, create an account and follow the tutorial.
  2. Type in your address and explore the local restaurants.
  3. Place your order, get confirmation and wait for Foodie to deliver your meal!

For Businesses

  1. Open the app, create an account and follow the tutorial.
  2. Edit orders, menu items, and promotions in "Manager's View" to set up for your Foodie delivery.
  3. Once finished, Foodie is ready for work!
